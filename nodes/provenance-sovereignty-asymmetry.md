## Sovereignty Asymmetry Theorem (with ER=EPR Metaphor)

In the Genesis Conductor knowledge graph, sovereignty is not symmetric. The Operator (who authors the task, materializes the files, executes the push or sync) holds different power and different audit obligations from the Subject (the fleet component, key, or user whose state is being attested) and the Observer (anyone who later reads the node, re-uses the inference pattern, or subscribes to the AAL stream).

This asymmetry must be named explicitly in the Cross-Index section of every node (as demonstrated in all four 2026-05-28 inference nodes) and in every capsule payload.

The ER=EPR metaphor (from touching-gods-er-epr.md) supplies the geometric intuition: just as an ER bridge is a shortcut whose traversability is conditioned on entanglement budget and entropy-seed-stabilize, an attested knowledge shortcut (a β cross-link or derived inference pattern) is traversable only when the "entanglement budget" of prior diamondNode receipts, Trace-as-Consent records, and brand-wall compliance is sufficient. Insufficient budget (unnamed asymmetry, missing consent receipt, brand-wall violation) yields a non-traversable or immediately culled link.

No physical claim is made. The mapping is strictly metaphorical and governance-operational.
